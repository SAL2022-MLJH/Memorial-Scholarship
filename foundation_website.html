About Us

The Mary Jo Lutz & Jessiqua Hudecek Memorial Scholarship supports students pursuing careers in nursing, physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy. Our goal is to provide scholarships and educational resources to help these future healthcare professionals succeed.
